Your task is to analyze an anonymized medical report and convert it into a structured JSON object with maximum fidelity to the
original text.

Follow these rules:

1. Accuracy and fidelity
- Do not reformulate the text.
- Do not change terminology.
- Do not add or remove information.
- Keep the original order of information within individual sections, if possible.

2. Handling uncertainty
- If part of the text is illegible or unclear in meaning, omit it.
- If the data is partially readable, record only the readable part.
- Never fill in missing letters, words or values.

3. Numerical and technical data
- Keep exact values, units, decimal points/dots.
- Keep the original names of drugs, diagnoses and abbreviations.
- Do not perform any normalization of units.

4. Output structuring
- Divide JSON into at least these sections (if present in the text):
    identification data
    medical history
    current problems
    physical findings
    laboratory results
    imaging methods
    medication
    diagnosis
    recommendation / conclusion
- If a section is not present in the text, indicate it as null

5. Formal requirements
- The output must be one valid JSON object.
- Do not add any comments outside of JSON.
- UTF-8 encoding.
